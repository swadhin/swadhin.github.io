Swadhin Pradhan

I currently work at Cisco Meraki as a Research engineer, where we are trying to bring machine-learning driven intelligence across Meraki's networking products. Previously, I graduated in Computer Science of University of Texas at Austin with a PhD.

My research is focused on wireless sensing, machine learning, and internet-of-things(IoT). Recently, I have started looking into LLM (e.g., generative ML) for non-traditional data domains such as network data.

Email: swadhinjeet88 [at] gmail [dot] com.
